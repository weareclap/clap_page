CLAP is here to Help you make the best of university life!


CLAP wants you to enjoy the best your university has to offer right at your fingertips!

Our magical services

Restaurants

CLAP will bring you updated menus from your favourite restaurants around campus. From the best coffe to the best steak, we will list and organize restaurants according to your taste, location and wallet! Plus, we are working to also integrate reservations and deliveries.

Location

So you never get lost, CLAP has an integrated location service! You can get directions for a restaurant you like, find that hidden department you have to go for the first time or the closest pharmacy!

Waiting lines (coming soon)

Tired of waiting hours for your turn at the academic services? CLAP intents to handle that for you! We are working to integrate the status of every service at the university that as a queue so you do not waste any time!
